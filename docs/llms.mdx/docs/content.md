# Welcome to Mate (/docs)



Mate is a cross-platform terminal app with built-in AI agent chat, a multi-file IDE, and a full library of MCP integrations — all accessible from any device on your network. Run it on your Mac, Windows, or Linux machine, then pair your phone to control everything remotely.

Think of it as your entire dev environment in one app: terminals, editors, AI assistants, and tool integrations, unified under a single interface that works across all your devices.

## What can you do with Mate? [#what-can-you-do-with-mate]

* **Run terminals** with full PTY support, tabs, and scrollback — locally or on a paired remote device
* **Chat with AI agents** like Claude Code, Codex, and Copilot, with tool use, conversation forking, and streaming responses
* **Edit code** in a built-in IDE with syntax highlighting for 100+ languages, a file tree, and git integration
* **Preview web apps** running on any paired device's localhost through a built-in reverse proxy
* **Transfer files** between devices with drag-and-drop simplicity
* **Talk instead of type** with built-in voice input supporting four STT engines including offline Whisper
* **Orchestrate agents** by running multiple AI sessions in parallel across your projects
* **Connect your tools** through MCP connectors — databases, APIs, cloud services, and more
* **Install skills** from the marketplace to add slash commands and specialized workflows to your agents
* **Sync across devices** — pair your phone, tablet, or another computer and access everything from anywhere on your network

## How it works [#how-it-works]

Your desktop runs Mate as the server. It owns all session state: terminals, projects, agent chats, and files. Your phone (or any other device on the same network) pairs with it and acts as a live mirror — you see and interact with the same sessions, in real time, from anywhere on your local network. No cloud. No accounts. Just your devices talking directly to each other.
